<p><ul><li>Push</li><li>Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Location-based advertising can be categorized into push and pull markets. In a push market, businesses proactively send targeted ads to consumers based on their geographical location, often through notifications or messages, aiming to capture immediate attention. In contrast, a pull market allows consumers to seek out promotional content themselves, typically through apps or websites that leverage their location to provide relevant offers. Both approaches aim to enhance consumer engagement by delivering contextually relevant advertisements based on users' whereabouts.</p></p>
