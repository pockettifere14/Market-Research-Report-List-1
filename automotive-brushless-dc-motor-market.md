<p><strong>The Automotive Brushless DC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Brushless DC Motor market features two primary types: inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. Inner rotor motors have their rotor positioned inside the stator, facilitating compact designs and high torque output, making them suitable for applications like electric vehicles. In contrast, outer rotor motors have the rotor encasing the stator, offering better cooling and stability, ideal for applications requiring higher efficiency and power. Both types are pivotal in advancing electric propulsion technology in the automotive sector.</p></p>
