<p><p>The Medical PE Tape market primarily consists of two types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is known for its flexibility, softness, and ability to conform to body contours, making it ideal for sensitive skin applications. On the other hand, HDPE offers superior strength and rigidity, providing durability and support in medical settings. Both types are widely used in wound care, surgical procedures, and adhesive applications, catering to varying needs in healthcare environments.</p></p>
