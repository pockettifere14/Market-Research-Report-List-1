<p><strong>The Emphysema Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrilobular emphysema</li><li>Panlobular emphysema</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emphysema treatment market is segmented into types based on the disease's presentation. Centriloabular emphysema primarily affects the upper lung regions and is often associated with smoking, leading to targeted treatments focusing on reducing inflammation and improving airflow. Panlobular emphysema, however, involves the enlargement of air spaces throughout the lungs and is commonly linked to genetic factors like alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency. Treatment strategies for both types may include bronchodilators, corticosteroids, and pulmonary rehabilitation tailored to their specific pathophysiology.</p></p>
