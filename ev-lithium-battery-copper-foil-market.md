<p><p>The EV lithium battery copper foil market features two main types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil, produced through an electrochemical process, offers high purity and uniform thickness, essential for efficient battery performance. In contrast, rolled copper foil is created by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, providing flexibility and cost advantages. Both types serve as critical conductors in lithium-ion batteries, contributing to energy storage efficiency and overall performance in electric vehicles.</p></p>
