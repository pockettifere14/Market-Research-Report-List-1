<p><p>Mini-Load Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) categorize into two main market types: Single Shuttle Systems and Dual Shuttle Systems. Single Shuttle Systems utilize one shuttle to transport goods between storage locations and retrieval points, providing efficiency for moderate throughput needs. In contrast, Dual Shuttle Systems deploy two shuttles, allowing simultaneous operations, which boosts efficiency and maximizes storage density. Both systems cater to various industries, enhancing inventory management and automating retrieval processes for small to medium-sized loads.</p></p>
