<p><p>Chromatography in cannabis testing is a critical analytical technique used to separate and identify compounds in cannabis products. Liquid chromatography (LC) is primarily used for compounds that are non-volatile, allowing for detailed analysis of cannabinoids and terpenes. Gas chromatography (GC), on the other hand, is ideal for volatile compounds, providing insights into terpene profiles and residual solvents. Other methods may include thin-layer chromatography (TLC) and supercritical fluid chromatography (SFC), each offering unique advantages for specific testing needs within the cannabis market.</p></p>
