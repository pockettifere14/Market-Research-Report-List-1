<p><p>Spectrophotometry market can be categorized into three main types: Pointer Type, Digital Type, and Others. Pointer Type spectrophotometers utilize analog displays with pointers to indicate absorbance or transmittance levels, providing a traditional measurement method. Digital Type spectrophotometers, on the other hand, offer precise readings on digital displays, enhancing accuracy and ease of use. Other market types may include advanced instruments with specialized functionalities, such as multi-wavelength analysis, portability, or automation features, catering to diverse applications in laboratories and industries.</p></p>
