<p><ul><li>Traditional Cataract Surgery</li><li>Laser-Assisted Cataract Surgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cataract surgery treatments mainly include traditional cataract surgery and laser-assisted cataract surgery. Traditional cataract surgery involves the manual phacoemulsification technique, where the cloudy lens is removed through a small incision and replaced with an artificial intraocular lens. In contrast, laser-assisted cataract surgery employs advanced femtosecond lasers for precise lens fragmentation and softening, enhancing accuracy and reducing recovery time. Both methods aim to restore clear vision, but the laser approach often offers improved precision and safety.</p></p>
