<p><p>The High Pressure Decorative Laminate (HPDL) market is categorized into two main types: "Universal" and "High Performance." Universal laminates are designed for general-purpose applications, offering a balance of aesthetics and durability, making them suitable for furniture, cabinetry, and wall coverings. On the other hand, High Performance laminates are engineered for specialized environments, providing enhanced resistance to scratches, chemicals, and moisture. This makes them ideal for demanding applications such as commercial kitchens, healthcare facilities, and high-traffic areas.</p></p>
