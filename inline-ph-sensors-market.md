<p><p>The inline pH sensors market includes several key types. Combination pH sensors consist of both electrode and reference elements in a single unit, providing accurate measurements with convenient installation. Differential pH sensors measure the potential difference between two electrodes, enhancing accuracy in fluctuating environments. Other types encompass specialized sensors designed for specific applications, such as high-temperature or high-pressure scenarios. This diversity in sensor types caters to various industries, including water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring optimal pH monitoring across different conditions.</p></p>
