<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market comprises two primary types: mechanical CPR machines and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent chest compressions by applying controlled pressure to the chest, ensuring effective blood circulation. Automated CPR machines streamline the resuscitation process, offering pre-set compression rates and depths while monitoring patient response. Both types enhance the quality of CPR, improve outcomes during cardiac events, and reduce the physical strain on healthcare providers during emergencies.</p></p>
