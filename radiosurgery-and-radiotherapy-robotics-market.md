<p><ul><li>X-ray Based</li><li>Gamma-ray Based</li><li>Proton-beam Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiosurgery and radiotherapy robotics involve advanced techniques using targeted radiation to treat tumors. X-ray based systems utilize high-energy X-rays for localized treatment, minimizing harm to surrounding tissues. Gamma-ray based systems employ gamma rays from radioactive isotopes, enabling precise tumor targeting. Proton-beam based systems use protons, allowing for controlled energy deposition, which is particularly effective for certain tumors. Together, these modalities enhance treatment efficacy, reduce side effects, and improve patient outcomes in cancer therapy.</p></p>
