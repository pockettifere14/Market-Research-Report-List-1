<p><p>The Augmented Bone Graft market comprises three main types: Allografts, Bone Graft Substitutes, and Cell-based Matrices. Allografts are harvested from donor bones and are used to promote healing and regeneration. Bone Graft Substitutes are synthetic or natural materials designed to mimic bone properties and support bone growth. Cell-based Matrices incorporate live cells to enhance tissue regeneration, providing a more biological approach to healing. Together, these options offer diverse solutions for various orthopedic and dental applications.</p></p>
