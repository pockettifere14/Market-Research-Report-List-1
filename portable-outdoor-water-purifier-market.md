<p><p>The portable outdoor water purifier market features various types designed for different needs. Extrusion water purifiers use a push mechanism to filter water efficiently. Pump water purifiers utilize a manual pump to draw water through a filtration system, ensuring effective purification. Suction water purifiers allow users to suck water directly from the source, making them convenient for on-the-go use. UV pen purifiers leverage ultraviolet light to sterilize water, providing a chemical-free option for safe drinking water in outdoor adventures.</p></p>
