<p><p>The Metal Organic Chemical Vapour Deposition (MOCVD) equipment market is segmented into three main heating types: RF Heating, Infrared Radiation Heating, and Resistance Heating. RF Heating Type utilizes radio frequency energy to achieve high temperatures, enhancing uniformity in film deposition. Infrared Radiation Heating Type relies on infrared light to directly heat substrates, providing rapid temperature control and energy efficiency. Resistance Heating Type employs resistive elements to generate heat, offering precise thermal management for various deposition processes, making it suitable for diverse applications in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
